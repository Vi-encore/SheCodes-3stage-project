You don't know where to donate?

In times of crisis, every contribution counts. With the ongoing war in Ukraine, many individuals around the world are eager to support the Ukrainians in their fight for peace and stability. However, knowing where to donate can be a challenge, especially for those who are unfamiliar with the process. If you're looking to make a difference but don't know where to start, here are list of some charity foundations.

Come Back Alive

Come Back Alive is both a charitable foundation dedicated to providing competent assistance to the Ukrainian military and a non-profit organization focused on security and defense sector analytics, as well as implementing projects for veteran rehabilitation through sports.
